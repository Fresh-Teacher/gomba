Who We Are

Gomba Next Generation Model Farm

With a vision rooted in transforming Uganda’s agricultural landscape, we are a leader in sustainable agribusiness and community empowerment. Spanning 600 acres, our farm is a hub of innovation, specializing in climate-resilient crop production, organic farming, livestock breeding, horticulture, and renewable energy. Our work supports the UN Sustainable Development Goals by driving food security, environmental stewardship, and inclusive economic growth. We’re committed to empowering marginalized communities—including youths, women, refugees, people with disabilities, and rural farmers—through hands-on training, assistive technologies. Awarded Uganda’s Best Agricultural Farm in 2020, we are a catalyst for change, forging a sustainable path that uplifts lives, revitalizes agriculture, and secures a prosperous future for all.
What We Offer

Premium Agricultural Products

Our farm offers a rich array of premium agricultural products, grown and managed to meet the highest quality and sustainability standards. Our crop production features robust staples like maize grain, along with organic bananas, coffee, and cocoa, ensuring a diverse supply for local and export markets. In livestock, we provide top-quality beef, dairy, and improved breeds of cattle, goats, and sheep. Our poultry sector delivers high-quality poultry products and improved breeds, while our aquaculture operations yield fresh, sustainably farmed fish. We also produce essential animal feeds, including hay and silage, supporting resilient agricultural practices across Uganda.

Award-Winning Excellence in Ugandan Agriculture

Recognized as Uganda’s Best Farmer in 2020, Gomba Farm has set a benchmark for excellence in the agricultural sector. Our farm’s growth and success are a testament to our dedication to quality, innovation, and community service. This prestigious award not only celebrates our achievements but also inspires us to continually elevate standards in farming practices, education, and environmental stewardship. We are committed to sustainable development, fostering rural job creation, and expanding opportunities for local communities.     Who We Are

    As Uganda's Best Agricultural Farm 2020, we are transforming the nation's agricultural landscape through our 600-hectare sustainable agribusiness hub. Our innovative approach drives food security and secures a prosperous future for all.

    Our Commitment

    At Gomba Next Generation Farm, we are committed to transforming Uganda's agricultural landscape through excellence and innovation. Our mission is to deliver premium, sustainable agro-products whilst empowering rural communities. We prioritise environmental stewardship, inclusive growth and community development, ensuring that each aspect of our operation contributes to a more resilient and prosperous agricultural future for Uganda.

    Core Values

    Our core values define our identity and guide our actions:

    • Sustainability: We champion sustainable agricultural practices and eco-friendly processing methods.
    • Innovation: We embrace innovation to enhance efficiency, quality, and product diversity.
    • Quality: We uphold the highest standards in processing to deliver superior agro-products.
    • Collaboration: We foster collaboration with farmers, stakeholders, and partners to build a robust agricultural ecosystem.

    Gomba Next Generation Farm is more than a facility; it's a commitment to excellence and a driving force in the agricultural landscape. Join us in shaping the future of agro-processing!
